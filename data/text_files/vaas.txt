The unpredictable and psychotic leader of the pirates on Rook Islands.
    Known for his iconic "Definition of Insanity" monologue to protagonist Jason Brody.
    Formerly a member of the Rakyat tribe before being corrupted by power and drugs.
    Works under the slave trader Hoyt Volker while terrorizing the local population.
    Defined by his intense emotional instability and philosophical view of chaos.
    Remains one of the most charismatic and feared villains in the Far Cry series.

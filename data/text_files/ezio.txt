Born in Florence, 1459, to the noble Auditore family.
    Witnessed the execution of his father and brothers, sparking his quest for revenge.
    Trained by his uncle Mario at Villa Auditore in Monteriggioni.
    Mastered the Hidden Blade, parkour, and social stealth.
    Defeated the Pazzi conspirators and eventually the Grand Master, Rodrigo Borgia.
    Rebuilt the Assassin Brotherhood in Rome to dismantle the Borgia influence.
    Travelled to Masyaf and Constantinople to uncover the secrets of Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad.
    Retired to a peaceful life in the Tuscan countryside with Sofia Sartor.
    Died in Florence in 1524, leaving behind a legacy of freedom and wisdom.

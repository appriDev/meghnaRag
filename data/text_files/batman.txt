Witnessed the tragic murder of his parents, Thomas and Martha Wayne, in a Gotham City alley.
    Travelled the world for years to master martial arts, detective skills, and forensic science.
    Adopted the persona of the Batman to strike fear into the hearts of criminals.
    Operates out of the Batcave beneath Wayne Manor with the help of Alfred Pennyworth.
    Utilizes high-tech gadgets, the Batmobile, and a strict code against killing.
    Serves as the founding member of the Justice League and the protector of Gotham.
